Social Engineering Project

The objective of this project was to perform a (simplified version of a) social engineering attack known as Watering Hole Attack. The watering hole attack got its name from geologic depressions in which water collects and where animals come to drink. By that analogy, a watering hole is simply a resource (like a website), under the control of an attacker, which many people (i.e., employees in an organization or customers in a coffee shop) visit or use. In this project, I assumed the role of a hacker who created a clone of a popular site (www.facebook.com) using SEToolkit, and through this clone site credentials of legitimate users are harvested...(Disclaimer: This was not used for malicious purposes).
